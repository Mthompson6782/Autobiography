# PART I: THE ORIGINS

---

## Overview
Baltimore. Childhood. Survival. The formation of two parallel identities — the kid who would one day read nuclear safety manuals and the kid who carried a pen as a weapon on Greenmount Avenue.

## Chapters

- **Chapter 1: The Résumé and The Rap Sheet** — The duality introduced. Who is Michael Thompson? The answer depends on which document you're reading.
- **Chapter 2: The Little Chickens and The Bleach** — Pauline Sutherland. The cruelty. The smell that never leaves.
- **Chapter 3: Pauline Sutherland** — The fierce protection of a tough grandmother. The Ms. Parsons incident.
- **Chapter 3.5: The Gentle Art and The Paratrooper** — Moving to Johnson Street. Mike's dad, the Army paratrooper, and the brutal lessons on how to end a street fight.
- **Chapter 4: Audrey Avenue** — The neighborhood. The culture. The ecosystem of the block.
- **Chapter 5: Mechanical Failures** — Systems start breaking. The machinery of childhood can only take so much stress before something gives.
