# Chapter 22: The Golden Handcuffs and the Great Escape

---

I went to Houston to be a Chief Engineer. I found out quickly that titles are cheap.

The **CETO** role (Chief Engineer and Technology Officer) sounded impressive on a business card. In reality, it was a sales grinder. They didn't want my "Intelligence and Guile"; they wanted me to shake hands, kiss babies, and close deals.

I hated it. It pulled me away from the technology, away from the problem-solving, and away from the things that actually stimulated my brain. I was burnt out within months.
